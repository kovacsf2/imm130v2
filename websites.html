My Three Favorite Websites

YouTube

YouTube
Ah, YouTube. The place where all the greatest content and video equivalent
of shovelware gets to be viewed by the masses. A simple site with an even
simpler design; compact, visually pleasing, easy to navigate. The classic
red, white, blue, and grey color scheme helps bring the whole site together.

Reddit

Reddit
Reddit: also known as 4chan's sober brother. Reddit is a culmination of
user's life stories, funny and serious videos, memes, and the occasional
post that makes you regret having eyes and learning to read. Much like
YouTube, Reddit uses the classic upvote/downvote system. View subreddits
of your favorite / most despised celebrity, play some 50/50 and risk
mental scarring in order to see either dogs or p0rn, or what some mild
karma.

Google

Google
The gateway to the entire entity that is the surface web. Google is the
Pandora's Box of the internet. Literally everything you need to find
(legal or not); all the information in the palm of your hands.
